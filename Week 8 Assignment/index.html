Welcome!

Good Doggie!

Chateau Chien welcomes all of out furry friends to bring your person and train them to keep you happy Wheater you are just a pup getting to know your new family, a young dog learning your first tricks, or a more mature dog who needs an etiquette refresher, we have a class for you.

Our trainers offer group classes where you can make some new friends and watch them train their people while you train yours. We also have private sessions if you are a bit shy or need something special.
